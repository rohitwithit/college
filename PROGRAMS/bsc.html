ELIGIBILITY ADMISSION PROCEDURE
A candidate who has passed H.S.C. (Class XII) from “Science stream with one of the subject Mathematics” and has obtained at least 50% (45% for reserved category) marks in Mathematics. Admissions will be given based on a merit list of marks obtained in H.S.C.

70% seats (28 seats) are reserved for the candidates passed from Maharashtra State Boards and 30% seats (12 seats) are reserved for candidates passed from other State Boards from other states of India. (Reservation of seats as per the rules of Government of Maharashtra for the candidates belonging to reserved category from the Maharashtra state).

Hostel Facilities are available for girls and boys on merit basis.

COURSE OBJECTIVES FUTURE CAREER PROSPECTS
  • To develop level of knowledge and understanding in the Computer Science domain along with the essential broad multidisciplinary related contexts.
  • To develop skills required to perform and accomplish tasks in Computer Science/IT and related technologies.
  • To acquire ability amongst students for application of gained Computer Science knowledge and skills.
  • To develop entrepreneurship skills and mindset so that students will be ready for employment in the IT industry.
  • To develop professional skills along with constitutional, humanistic, ethical, and moral values with respect to Computer Science domain
  • To introduce students to various domains of research in the field of Computer Sciences and Information Technology
  • Full Stack Development
  • Cyber Security
  • Artificial Intelligence & ML
  • Web Development
  • Cloud Computing
  • Data Science
  • IOT
  • Block Chain Technology
  • Thrust Areas of Computer Sciences
