Eligibility Admission Procedure
General Criteria:
Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Statistics, Mathematics, Engineering, or a related field. Basic Programming or mathematics background is preferred.

The candidate must have secured at least 50% (or equivalent CGPA) marks at graduation (45% for reserved category candidates from Maharashtra state). 		Online Centralized Admission Procedure (CAP) for admission to this course is carried out by the university in the month of June.

Course Objectives Future Career Prospects
  • The course will provide fundamental conceptual knowledge about Data Analytics, Data Science and Big Data Processing.
  • The course would cover core applied aspects of mathematics and statistics required for AI/ML and Deep Learning.
  • It would provide insight into Data Analytics techniques, LLM Models and Deep Learning.
  • The course will improve programming skills and provide exposure to various libraries and frameworks such as TensorFlow, Keras, Tableau, Jupyter, HADOOP and SPARK.
  • It provides exposure to LLM and Deep Learning through NLP specialization. It also provides specialization in DIP.
  • It prepares students for roles like Data Scientists, Analysts, and AI/ML Engineers.
  • Become a Data Scientist, Data Analyst, or Machine Learning Engineer in leading tech companies.
  • Contribute to cutting-edge research in artificial intelligence, predictive analytics, and big data technologies.
  • Join industries like healthcare, finance, retail, and logistics to solve real-world problems using data-driven solutions.
  • Develop intelligent systems and data products to support strategic decision-making.
  • Work in academia or research labs to advance knowledge in statistical modeling and data mining.
  • Start your own data consultancy or analytics-based startup to offer innovative business solutions.
  • Collaborate with international institutions on global data challenges and research projects.
  • Play a key role in data governance, ethics, and policy-making in the digital economy.
