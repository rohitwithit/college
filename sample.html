Computer Infrastructure

The School of Computer Science has Departmental Library having 3019 number of titles and 205 number of volumes of national and international journals on Computer Science. Also, the school has 08 laboratories equipped with workstations and necessary software programs. Besides, there is a separate instructor cabin in each laboratory. All machines are networked.
