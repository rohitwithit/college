Computer Infrastructure

The School of Computer Science has Departmental Library having 3019 number of titles and 205 number of volumes of national and international journals on Computer Science. Also, the school has 08 laboratories equipped with workstations and necessary software programs. Besides, there is a separate instructor cabin in each laboratory. All machines are networked. The following table gives details of

Sr. No. Particulars Computer facility Available as on date
1. No. Of Computer Terminals 150
2. Hardware Specification HP Z200 Workstation, Intel Xeon 2.40 GHz, 8GB RAM, 320GB HDD
3. No. Of Terminals on LAN/WAN There is provision of 225 Network nodes in the Department. All nodes are on the LAN
4. Printers 25 (Laser/IP based Network Dot Matrix/Ink Jet)
5. Internet Accessibility 1 Gbps leased line connectivity under NKN connectivity is provided in the Laboratories.
6. Wi-Fi Connectivity The building of School of Computer Sciences is fully Wi-Fi enabled
